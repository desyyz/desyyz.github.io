My Process

1. Understand

I begin by understanding the brand, audience, and purpose of the website. Design decisions should always support meaning and usability.

2. Design

I focus on layout, spacing, and motion to create calm and intuitive interfaces before writing code.

3. Build

I write clean, readable HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with performance and maintainability in mind.

4. Refine

I test responsiveness, improve animations, and refine details to ensure the site feels polished and human-made.
